About the NASA Weather Probability Dashboard

The NASA Weather Probability Dashboard was created to make climate data more accessible to researchers, students, and anyone curious about global weather trends. By combining historical records with modern datasets, this platform provides a unique window into how our climate has evolved since 1763.
